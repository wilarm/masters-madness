How It Works

The Tournament Structure:

  • Selection: Select 1 golfer from each of the 8 groups.
  • Scoring: Your team’s scores are aggregated. The scores of your 4 lowest-scoring golfers count towards your total.
  • The Challenge: If more than 4 of your golfers miss the cut, your team will automatically count the score required to make the weekend cut as a replacement score.
    (For example, if only 3 of your players make the cut, your team’s 4th score will be filled with the score necessary to make the weekend cut.)

Making Your Picks:

Select your team from 8 groups based on performance potential. Each group was created based on odds to win the tournament. Selections can be made at the bottom of the page (Max 2 entries per participant). You will receive an email confirmation acknowledging your picks and can change them at any time up until the deadline.

Entry Submission:

  • Deadline: ~5am MT, Thursday, April 7th.
  • Entry Fee: $100 per team, with a maximum of two teams per participant.
  • Payment Methods: Venmo (@Drake-Fages) or alternative arrangements via contact with Drake @ 951-818-7517.

Payouts and Prizes

  • Total Prize Money: $1,500.
  • 1st Place: $1,000
  • 2nd Place: $400
  • 3rd Place: $100

Prize money is guaranteed through private underwriting and will be awarded directly to winners. Tiebreakers are in place to ensure a fair and competitive tournament.
